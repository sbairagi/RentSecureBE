# RentSecure Overview

RentSecure is a smart property management platform.

Primary Users:
- PG Owners
- Hostel Managers
- Building Lessors

Core Features:
- Property Management
- Rent Tracking
- Rent Agreements
- Police Verification
- Subscription Plans
- Add-ons
- WhatsApp Reminders
- AI Financial Health Check

Critical Rules:
- Subscription expiry blocks all features.
- Add-on limits are independent.
- Property history PDFs are important.
- Rent agreement revocation occurs after missed payments.
