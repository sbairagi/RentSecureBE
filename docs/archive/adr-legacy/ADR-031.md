# Manual UPI Payments as Year 1 Payment Strategy

## Status
Accepted

## Date
2026-07-14

## Context
RentSecure requires a payment flow for Year 1 that operates within a strict monthly budget of ₹2,000–3,000 INR. Payment gateway providers (Razorpay, Cashfree) charge transaction fees that, combined with AWS infrastructure costs, would exceed the budget constraints. Additionally, a solo founder cannot manage payment gateway compliance, disputes, and settlements during the bootstrap phase.

## Decision
Year 1 uses a completely free manual UPI-based payment flow:
- Owners register UPI ID, QR Code, and Bank Account
- Tenants pay directly via any UPI app
- Tenants upload UTR and payment proof
- Owners manually verify and approve/reject
- Rent Receipt PDF generated and emailed on approval

Payment gateway adapters (Razorpay, Cashfree) are implemented but disabled behind feature flags.

## Consequences
- Zero transaction fees
- No payment gateway compliance burden
- No webhook handling complexity
- Owner maintains full control over funds
- Manual verification overhead scales with transaction volume
- Requires owners to be actively involved in payment verification
- Triggers Stage 2 migration at 500+ payments/month or 10+ hours/week verification time
